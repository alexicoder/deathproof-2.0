Introducing Chris Derry

A Soul Born to Groove

Meet Chris Derry, the mastermind behind the electrifying persona of Deathproof Audio. Hailing from the vibrant town of Swindon, UK, Chris is a solo DJ on a mission to conquer hearts and dance floors with his unique and soul-stirring music.

From a young age, Chris knew that music coursed through his veins. Fueled by an unwavering passion for beats and melodies, he embarked on a journey of musical exploration, seeking to forge a sound that would captivate and move his audiences.

The Magic of Deathproof Audio

Fusing Genres, Igniting Souls

The essence of Deathproof Audio's style lies in the alchemy of genres. Fearlessly blending psydub, drum and bass, jungle, and a myriad of other influences, Chris creates a spellbinding fusion that is entirely his own. His sets become transformative experiences, taking listeners on a breathtaking ride through a world of sonic wonders.

When Chris takes the stage, a whirlwind of energy envelops the crowd. The dance floor becomes a realm of unity as the music weaves a powerful connection between the DJ and his audience. With every beat, he pours his heart and soul into the performance, leaving an indelible mark on those who bear witness to the magic of his music.

Crafting Sonic Landscapes

The Creative Sanctuary of Deathproof Audio

Beyond the limelight, Chris is a tireless studio enthusiast, a connoisseur of sonic craftsmanship. In his creative sanctuary, he tirelessly experiments and refines his tracks, seeking to push the boundaries of sound and create compositions that resonate on a profound level. His studio endeavors are a testament to his dedication and a testament to his unyielding pursuit of musical excellence.

The Dance Floor Connection

Celebrating the Power of Sound

But it's not just about the fame or applause for Chris. His true reward lies in the euphoria he witnesses on the faces of his audience, the shared joy that fills the air when music becomes the universal language of celebration. For him, music is an expression of life, a conduit of emotion, and a bond that unites people like nothing else.

Through his enchanting beats and magnetic stage presence, Chris Derry, a.k.a. Deathproof Audio, continues to redefine the boundaries of music. With an unwavering commitment to spreading his love for music, he embarks on a quest to touch the souls of countless music lovers worldwide. So come, join him on this thrilling musical journey, and let the power of sound transport you to a realm of pure musical bliss. As Deathproof Audio reigns supreme, hearts will throb, feet will move, and music will triumph in its most glorious form.
